Egg Rolls


About the recipe

Crispy egg rolls filled with seasoned pork, cabbage, and carrots are a fabulous side dish to serve alongside stir-fry dishes. These homemade egg rolls are almost identical to what you'd get at a Chinese restaurant! Serve warm with egg roll sauce or soy sauce if desired.


Ingredients

  • half pound ground pork
  • half teaspoon ground ginger, or more to taste
  • half teaspoon garlic powder, or more to taste
  • one ounces shredded carrots
  • one cup shredded cabbage
  • one tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • one tablespoon water
  • one tablespoon peanut oil for frying, or as needed
  • four (7 inch square) egg roll wrappers
  • one tablespoon sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees C (220 degrees C).

  2. Roll out pizza crust and place on a pizza pan or baking sheet. Spread a thin layer of hummus over the crust. Arrange sliced peppers and broccoli over the hummus, and top with shredded cheese.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and cheese is melted in the center, 10 to 15 minutes. Slice and serve.
